Dikshant Sharma

I'm a Research Assistant at the Vision, Language and Learning Group, IIT Jodhpur, advised by Dr. Anand Mishra. My research focuses on multimodal, scene-text aware image–text retrieval, with an emphasis on building and evaluating retrieval models for multilingual settings.

I completed my M.Sc. in Computer Science (AI & ML specialization) from the Central University of Rajasthan (CGPA: 8.73/10), and my B.C.A. from the University of Rajasthan (73.83%). I qualified UGC-NET JRF (CS & Applications) and GATE (CSE). I also served as an Emergency Reviewer for ICVGIP 2025.

I work primarily with Python and PyTorch, and I frequently use NumPy, Pandas, scikit-learn, Matplotlib, Git/GitHub, and LaTeX in my research workflow.
